Diploma in Nursing and Midwifery

The Diploma in Nursing and Midwifery program at Prime School of Nursing and Midwifery is designed to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to provide high-quality healthcare. This course covers **maternal health, pediatric care, pharmacology, medical-surgical nursing, and community health.

Students will gain hands-on experience through clinical rotations, laboratory simulations, and practical hospital training to ensure they are fully prepared for professional healthcare environments.
